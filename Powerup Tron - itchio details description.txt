Powerup Tron

Experience a modern take on a classic arcade concept! Guide your light trail through a 3D world in this snake game inspired by the light cycles of Tron.

Achieve higher scores to unlock a variety of powerups that add new twists to the gameplay. Can you collect them all?

This project was developed as an experiment in AI-assisted coding, built entirely using Google's Gemini 2.5 Pro within the Cursor editor.

If you enjoy Powerup Tron, consider supporting the project to help future development!
